How to play
The "next wave" button responcebal on starting the wave when you click on it the wave will start and the button will disepear until all of the orcs are gone. note that in evry wave there are more orcs comming with diffrent module.
To add a new tower you shoud click on the tower you want to add and then move the mouse to the place you want to put it in. after that the range will show. and to put it you have to click on the place.
